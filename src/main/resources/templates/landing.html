About EventHub

For Students

EventHub makes it easy to discover what's happening around campus. Whether it's a tech talk, a social club gathering, or a career fair, you can explore upcoming events in one place. RSVP online, get reminders, and never miss an opportunity to connect, learn and have fun.

For Clubs / Organisations

EventHub gives clubs and student organisations the tools to share and manage events with ease. Create and publish events in minutes, track RSVPs, and gather feedback from attendees. With EventHub, reaching students and building engagement across campus has never been simpler.
